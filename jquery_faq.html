Redwood National Park

  • Redwood National and State Parks is in northern California just below the Oregon border.
  • Visitor centers are open from 9 am to 5 pm each day in summer, and 9 am to 4 pm in autumn to spring.
  • There is a $35.00 per night/per site fee to camp.
  • Some redwoods live to 2,000 years.
